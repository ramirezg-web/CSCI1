Problem #2: Calculating Profits

You work for a manufacturer, and have been asked to calculate the total profit and profit margin made on the sales of a product.

Write a profit calculator that does the following:

  1. Ask the user for the cost price per unit (in dollars).
  2. Ask the user for the sales price per unit (in dollars).
  3. Also, ask for the starting inventory sold.
  4. Compute the total profit made—rounded to the nearest dollar.
    • Profit = Total Sales - Total Cost
  5. Calculate the profit margin by subtracting the cost price from the sales price and dividing by the sales price.

Sample input/output:

Enter sales prices: 45.00
Enter inventory sold: 1200

You made a profit of $14,796.
